README!

          I created this app to help me with one of the international relations courses I took at the University of Washington which required me to
          read the international section of The New York Times every day. While the summaries generated are pretty rough, they generally get the job done.
          

          To use this app simply copy the raw text of a news article (i.e. one from the NY Times, The Economist, The Wall Street Journal, ect.) and the app will try to summarize it to a user-specified number of sentences. This app is still a work in progress and has many bugs that need to be worked out.
          

          WARNING: Any copied text the app takes must only be the text in the article. Text from things like ads, website elements will cause noise in the summarizing algorithm.
